## Remarks

> **Remark 1:** Here, 'real analysis' means the so-called 'real analysis' in China, which is actually usually a graduate-level measure theory course in American universities. For example, in UC Berkeley, such contents are covered in MATH 202A – MATH 202B. The so-called 'real analysis' in the U.S. is delivered as a series of 'mathematical analysis' courses in China, which is usually a 3-term series. In SUSTech, each one within the series is worth 5 U.S. credits.
>
